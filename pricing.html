how the money works.

you pay a flat rate for access to the bench. find someone you like, email them directly. work out terms between yourselves. we're not involved in the hire.

questions, asked.

do i need an account?

no. you pay through stripe, we email you a link, you bookmark it. that's the whole login. no passwords to forget.

what happens after my pass expires?

the link stops working. the bench goes back behind the wall. grab another pass when you need one — most agencies cycle the week pass when a pitch lands.

can i share my access link?

technically yes. you'll have one link. honor system applies. if you're a 30-person shop sharing one $49 pass, the monthly is probably better for you anyway.

do you take a cut when i hire someone?

never. you contract directly. we don't see the deal, don't take a fee, don't get involved.

what's the refund policy?

email us within 24 hours of buying if it's not what you expected. we'll refund without a meeting.

how do you keep the bench fresh?

every creative confirms availability monthly and refers three peers. anyone who doesn't, drops off. so what you see is who's actually working — not who applied two years ago.
